Overview

Dr. Chunfeng Song received the BSc degree from QiLu University of Technology in 2012, the MSc degree from North China Electric Power University in 2016, and the PhD degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2019. Since December 2019, he has joined the Center for Research on Intelligent Perception and Computing (CRIPAC), National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition (NLPR), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) as an Associate Professor. He has published several conference and journal papers on IEEE CVPR/AAAI/BMVC/PR. His current research focuses on person identification, image segmentation, and data clustering.

Research Area

Computer vision, biometrics, face preprocessing
